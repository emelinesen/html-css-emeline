Our Story

Norwegian weather doesn't wait — and neither should you. Our rain jackets are engineered to handle everything the sky throws at you, whether you're paddling through a fjord, hiking a ridge in a downpour, or just refusing to let the forecast ruin your weekend.

Fully waterproof. Windproof. Breathable. Our jackets use sealed seams and premium membrane technology so moisture stays where it belongs — outside. No leaks, no soaked layers, no excuses.

From first-timer kayakers to seasoned trail runners, we outfit people who choose to go out anyway.
